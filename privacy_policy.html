Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

1. Introduction

Welcome to Expense Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with transparency and care.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Expense Tracker web application. By creating an account, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Account Information: Email address, phone number, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Profile Data: First name, last name, date of birth, gender, and home address (all optional).
  • Expense Records: Date, category, and amount for each expense you log.
  • Preferences: App settings like theme preference, stored locally in your browser (localStorage).
We do not collect: Payment card details, bank account numbers, or any real financial credentials.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the purpose of operating the Expense Tracker application:

  • To create and manage your user account securely.
  • To store, display, and analyse your expense data.
  • To allow you to generate PDF and CSV spending reports.
  • To personalise your experience (profile details, theme preferences).
  • To communicate essential service notices (if contact is enabled).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored in a locally-hosted MySQL database. We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using PHP's password_hash() (bcrypt) — never stored in plain text.
  • All database queries use PDO prepared statements to prevent SQL injection.
  • PHP sessions are used for secure authentication management.
  • Input is sanitised with htmlspecialchars() to prevent XSS attacks.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data within the application:

  • Access: View all your profile information on the Profile page at any time.
  • Correction: Update any of your details via the Update Profile page.
  • Export: Download all your expense records as a CSV or PDF from the Expense History page.
  • Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Update Profile page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the Contact Us page. We aim to respond within 3 business days.

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
